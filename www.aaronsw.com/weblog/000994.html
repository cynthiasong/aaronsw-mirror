San Francisco Moment

On the way to the car we saw a really broken car speeding along with half the SFPD following behind it.

In the car on the way to the Caltrain we saw the police cars taking up a whole street on the left (presumably surrounding the broken car).

On the Caltrain back to Palo Alto, the folks behind me had a long discussion about the character dynamics of Homestar Runner.
