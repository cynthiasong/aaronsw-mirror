iCal!

So today Apple released iCal, the Internet calendaring component of their application suite. It imports straight from Entourage, lets you publish calendars to the Web (here’s my travel calendar) and subscribe to it with one click (although they invented a new URI scheme…).

It integrates with Address Book (drag user to news item to invite them) and Mail (uses it to send invitations). Here at the OpenForce office, I’m converting all the Entourage users over.
